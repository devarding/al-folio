Research

My research employs multi-scale modeling techniques, including large-eddy simulations and kilometer-scale storm-resolving models, alongside advanced remote sensing technologies like lidar and radar, complemented by cutting-edge deep learning methodologies. Building on this overarching goal of understanding the integrated surface-PBL-cloud system, I have developed several interconnected research topics.

Remote Sensing of PBL and Cloud-Land Coupling
Roles of Aerosol in Surface-PBL-Cloud System
Process-level Mechanisms of Cloud-Land Coupling
Representation of Cloud Coupling in Reanalysis Data and Multiscale Models
Deep Learning Driven Simulation of Surface-PBL-Cloud System
